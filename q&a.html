Frequently Asked Questions

☞ Does each and every student get an individual feedback on how they answered the questions?

Yes, they do. Every student receives a unique feedback according to their performances on question papers. Futhermore, the overall performances will be sent to their parents or guardians.

☞ Are the questions available in multiple languages?

Yes, every question is available in Sinhala, English and Tamil languages.

☞ Can the users download their answer sheets after submitting?

No, currently they can't downlaod their own answer sheets. However, our team is working on that matter.

☞ Do the users have access to model paper questions rather than past paper questions?

Yes, but a few questions are still around. We'll be adding more questions with the partnership of your favourite educational institutes and tutors.

☞ As parents, how can we keep record of our children?

Month after month or week after week, our team will be sending an email or a text message to a link that shows your child's progress
